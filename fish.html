fishmarket

. “Everybody is a genius. But if you judge a fish by its ability to climb a tree, it will live its whole life believing that it is stupid.” - Albert Einstein.

In search of food, I lost my family, came into new world. Where number of my knows struggling for water and space. And peoples around me pointing each of us , first few minutes I thought that if I jump off from this crowded bucket then I will back to my family, My home ,where I swim with my babies and got caught when I was searching food for them. Now what for I m regretting? For not taking risk for food orr got caught for food.
I made myself strong and took a large jump , hopeing that I will fall on the land of fluid, I will fall on river and swim again where I again see my babies. But within a second I got a hard hit of mudddy land , mixture of bloods and soil. Bloods of my known mates. I just lost my mind and started facing huge shortage of water, I barely breathe.
