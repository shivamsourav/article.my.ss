LOST-
Searching of Family

“The greatest thing in family life is to take a hint when a hint is intended-and not to take a hint when a hint isn’t intended.” — Robert Frost.

You made him your family. You brought his life with some paper money. You cared , love and started naming him. But i have seen that tiny drop in his eyes for not understanding what's going on. Where is his mom? Where is he? Where is his family?
Every breeds of puppy lost his family and kept under a price tag. At very early age he lost his mom , his family and came under the stage of pets.
Yes you take cared soo much. You will going to love him soo much. After some day he will forgot his family. But the pain what he is feeling in that early time, we just paid some paper money for that pain. You made him your family by snatching his own family. We talk about consents of each other before taking something. Did you ask him what he wants? Is he happy to leave his family? Is he agreed to be your family member? Who gave you right to buy someone's life, someone's family?
