Female- Mirror
of Society

"Don't think about making women fit the world — think about making the world fit women.”
-Gloria Steinem.

She is not the way you see. She is not the thing you need. She is not the food you eat. She is not the dress you wear. She is not the way you judge . Never judge any girl with their appearance, accent of speaking and their dressing sense. She not the thing you buy and bargain. Stop underestimating her with their potential
Stop underestimating that she can't do that. Stop limiting her. Stop judging her . Stop making hints for her any action. Stop identifying her name under their father or husband names. She is a bird who can fly and cover entire limits. She is the sky who can shine so that every night gets washed off. She is the flower who created colors in this planet. Beauty defines from her. She is unique. Unity represented by her. Equality represented by her. Devotion flows in her. She is the creator of life.She is the creator of everything
She is SHE.
-Shivam Sourav
